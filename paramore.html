About Us

Coldplay, British rock group whose melodic piano-driven anthems lifted it to the top of the pop music world in the early 21st century.
Coldplay was formed in 1998 at University College, London,
with the pairing of pianist-vocalist Chris Martin (b. March 2, 1977, Exeter, England)
and guitarist Jonny Buckland
